Frequently Asked

Questions worth asking.

What is sovereign AI?+

Sovereign AI means running AI on infrastructure you control — your hardware, your data, your governance — rather than depending on external cloud providers. It keeps knowledge, models, and decisions aligned with your organization's goals and obligations.

Why local AI?+

Local AI keeps sensitive data inside your walls, removes per-use cloud costs, and gives you continuity that doesn't depend on a vendor's pricing or policy changes. Modern open models make this practical today.

Do I need dedicated hardware?+

Often a single well-specified machine is enough to start — from a compact workstation for a small team to a dedicated server for larger deployments. We right-size hardware to your workload during design.

Can local and cloud AI coexist?+

Yes. Many organizations adopt a hybrid approach — sensitive workloads stay local and sovereign, while non-sensitive tasks use cloud where it makes sense. We help you draw that line deliberately.

What industries benefit most?+

Professional services, legal, healthcare, research, NGOs, and mission-driven and public-sector organizations — anywhere knowledge is sensitive and control matters.

How long do engagements take?+

An assessment can take a few weeks; a first sovereign system can be running shortly after. Larger programs run longer. We scope every engagement to your goals and constraints.
